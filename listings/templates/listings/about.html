AI-powered construction cost estimation using Deep Learning.

The Construction Cost Prediction Module is an intelligent system that estimates building construction costs using an Artificial Neural Network (ANN). This approach captures complex relationships between construction parameters and total cost.

Users provide the following construction details:

  • State and City
  • Built-up Area (sq.ft)
  • Number of Bedrooms (BHK) & Bathrooms
  • Age of the Building

The input data is preprocessed and passed through multiple hidden layers of the ANN model, which learns non-linear patterns from historical construction cost data and produces an accurate cost prediction.

The system also maintains a history of predictions, allowing users to review previous estimates for better planning and analysis.

Domain: Artificial Intelligence & Deep Learning

Technologies Used: Python, Artificial Neural Networks (ANN), TensorFlow, Scikit-learn, Django, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap
